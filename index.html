How it works

  1. Color decorrelation — separates brightness from color (G, R−G, B−G). Channels become independent, each with lower entropy.
  2. Adaptive prediction — for each row, 6 predictors are tested (none, left, above, average, Paeth, MED). The winner is chosen per-row — residuals are tiny.
  3. Entropy coding — the near-zero residuals are deflated. Same backend as PNG, but our residuals are 20–40% smaller.
