How it works

  1. Decorrelate — G, R−G, B−G. Each channel becomes independent.
  2. Predict — 6 filters tested per row. The winner makes residuals tiny.
  3. Deflate — zlib compresses the near-zero residuals.

The hex editor below the image shows the actual compressed bytes. Edit any byte and click the image — the decoder runs on your modified data. Corrupted bytes produce real glitch art.
